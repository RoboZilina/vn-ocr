🏮 Studio Guide

Everything you need to get crisp reads from your visual novel

🚀 Quick Start — 30-Second Setup

  1. Click Select Window Source and choose your VN window.
  2. Drag a rectangle around the dialogue text area in the mirrored preview.
  3. OCR runs instantly — text appears below and is copied to clipboard.

Essential Controls:

  • 🔄 RE-CAPTURE: Manually re-scan the same region anytime.
  • AUTO Button: Enable hands-free continuous reading (next to RE-CAPTURE).
  • Debug Thumbnail: Shows the exact capture sent to OCR — use it to check scaling and clarity.
  • Scaling & Engine: Adjust in the header for best results on small or complex fonts.
  • TTS & History: Hear lines spoken aloud (🔊) or browse the sidebar for past logs.

The OCR Engine reads the text. The Image Processing Mode prepares the image for the engine. You can mix and match them depending on the VN.

🔮 Image Processing Modes

These modes control how the image is prepared for the OCR engine (they are NOT the OCR models):

  • Adaptive: Best all-rounder. Converts to black/white automatically. Start here.
  • Multi-Pass 🔥: Runs 5 different preprocessing combos and picks the winner by confidence + Japanese character density. Slower but smarter.
  • Last Resort 🚨: Nuclear option — 7 passes. Isolates the textbox, reconstructs character strokes, then tries every upscale & binarize combination. Use when nothing else works.
  • Contrast ⬛: Hard black/white. Best for flat, clean backgrounds.
  • Grayscale 🔲: Preserves tonal detail. Good for colorful or gradient backgrounds.
  • Raw 📷: No processing. Use to test or for very clean high-res text.

🔍 Scaling & OCR Engine

Adjust the Scaling slider to upscale the capture before OCR:

  • 1x – 2x: Good for standard 1080p games with clear, large fonts.
  • 3x – 4x: Use for 720p games or tiny packed dialogue boxes.

Choose your OCR Engine (Language Model) for accuracy vs. speed:

  • jpn_best: Highest accuracy. Larger download, slower first load.
  • jpn: Balanced. Good default if jpn_best feels slow.
  • jpn_fast: Fastest load. Slightly lower accuracy.
  • jpn_vert 縦書き: For traditional top-to-bottom vertical text layouts.

👁️ Autonomous Eye (Auto-Capture)

Use the AUTO button next to RE-CAPTURE to enable hands-free OCR.

  • AUTO: ON — Indicator turns green
  • AUTO: OFF — Indicator turns gray
  • Clicking toggles automation instantly
  • Reflects the global state also found in the side menu

How it works:

  • Scans the selected region every 500ms for pixel-level changes.
  • Waits 800ms after stabilization to avoid mid-transition frames.
  • Works best with a tight capture region around the dialogue text.

🔊 Text-to-Speech

  • Select a Japanese voice from the TTS Voice dropdown in the header.
  • Click 🔊 next to the latest line to hear it spoken aloud.
  • Every history entry also has its own 🔊 button for replaying past lines.
  • Select 🔇 TTS Off to disable speech entirely.

💡 Pro Tips

  • 🚨 Panic Button: One click instantly switches to Last Resort mode and re-fires OCR — no menu diving needed.
  • Clipboard: Every successful OCR result is auto-copied to your clipboard.
  • History: The sidebar logs up to 100 lines, restored on next session. Use 📋 to copy any past line.
  • Zoom in: If the debug thumbnail looks blurry or tiny — raise the Scaling slider before re-capturing.
